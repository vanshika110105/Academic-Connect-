Advocacy for student welfare through regular feedback mechanisms, addressing concerns, and liaising with university authorities.

The journey of the Hostel Committee has not been without its share of challenges. As Chitkara University continues to grow and evolve, the Hostel Committee remains committed to its mission of fostering a vibrant and inclusive community within the hostels. Conclusion:

The history of the Hostel Committee at Chitkara University is a testament to the power of student leadership and community engagement. From its humble beginnings to its present stature, the organization has remained true to its ethos of service and solidarity, shaping the fabric of campus life and leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of all who call the hostels their home.
