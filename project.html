Chitkara University

Chitkara University is a private university located in Rajpura, Punjab and in Baddi,himachalpradesh India. It offers undergraduate programs, post-graduate program and doctoral programs in fields of engineering, management, pharmacy, health sciences, nursing, hospitality, art & design and education.
