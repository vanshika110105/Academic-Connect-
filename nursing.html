About Nursing Program

The Nursing program at Chitkara University prepares students for a rewarding career in healthcare. With a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, our program equips students with the expertise and compassion needed to excel in the field of nursing.

Curriculum

The curriculum includes a combination of classroom lectures, laboratory sessions, and clinical rotations. Some of the key courses offered in the program include:

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing
  • Pharmacology
  • Maternity and Pediatric Nursing
  • Mental Health Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing

Faculty

Our faculty members are experienced healthcare professionals who are dedicated to providing quality education and mentorship to our students. They bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise from their diverse backgrounds in nursing practice and research.

Facilities

Your College offers state-of-the-art facilities to support the learning and development of nursing students. Some of the facilities include:

  • Simulation Labs with high-fidelity mannequins
  • Clinical Skills Lab for hands-on training
  • Library with extensive resources on nursing and healthcare
  • Student Support Services for academic and personal guidance

Why Choose Nursing at Chitkara University

At Your College, we are committed to providing a comprehensive nursing education that prepares students to meet the challenges of modern healthcare. Our program emphasizes critical thinking, evidence-based practice, and compassionate care, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to make a difference in the lives of patients and communities.

Admissions

Admissions to the Nursing program at Chitkara University are open to students who have completed their higher secondary education (10+2) with a strong background in science. Admission criteria may vary depending on the college's regulations and guidelines.
