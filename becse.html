Course Details

The Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering (BE-CSE) program at Chitkara University is a four-year undergraduate program that provides students with a strong foundation in computer science and engineering principles. The program is designed to prepare students for a career in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and engineering.

Syllabus

  • First Year: Basic Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Programming in C, Data Structures, and Algorithms
  • Second Year: Computer Organization, Operating Systems, Database Management Systems, Software Engineering, and Web Technology
  • Third Year: Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Computer Networks, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Mining
  • Fourth Year: Cloud Computing, Big Data Analytics, Internet of Things, and Final Year Project

Faculty

Our faculty members are experienced professionals with a deep knowledge of computer science and engineering principles. They are committed to providing students with a high-quality education that prepares them for a successful career in the field.
